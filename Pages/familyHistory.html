About the tech survey

Currently over 1400 responses, the ongoing 2024 survey aims to measure attitudes towards mental health in the tech workplace, and examine the frequency of mental health disorders among tech workers.

Demographics Overview

Overview

From the parallel sets visualization, it shows the dataset pertains to individuals segmented by their employment status in tech companies, geographical location, age, and gender.

The overview presents an opportunity to delve deeper into the nuances of how different demographics within the tech sector engage with mental health services and their specific needs.

Territory

The majority of the individuals work in tech companies, particularly in the United States, which has the highest representation. The majority from the United States of America are also currently working in medium or large companies.

Since the majority are from the United States of America, we zoomed in further to identify cities that had the most respondents.

California and Illinois had the highest number of respondents with 147 from California and 136 from Illinois.
