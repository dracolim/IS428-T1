Demographics Overview

From the parallel sets visualization, it shows the dataset pertains to individuals segmented by their employment status in tech companies, geographical location, age, and gender.

The overview presents an opportunity to delve deeper into the nuances of how different demographics within the tech sector engage with mental health services and their specific needs.

Territory

The majority of the individuals work in tech companies, particularly in the United States, which has the highest representation, suggesting a significant focus on the mental health within the tech industry there.

Age

There's also a diverse age distribution, with a concentration in the 31-50 age range, indicating that mid-career professionals are the most represented in this dataset.

Gender

The gender distribution leans heavily towards males, followed by females and a smaller proportion of non-binary individuals. This could imply a gender disparity within the mental health conversation in the tech industry, with potential implications for targeted wellness programs or resource allocation.
